Do Best Movie For You

VoilàNAM TV presents a new visons in online cinema streaming content promoting the Vietnamese people and culture. We create original feature and short films, music shows, music videos, as well as travel shows which promotes a positive Vietnamese image, showcasing further diversity on screens across the world. Audiences around the world can download the Voilà NAM TV app on their media device of their choosing, in order to watch our exciting entertainment using Google Chromecast on any media device.
We at Voilà NAM TV want to bring high quality entertainment to viewers like the short horror film “Satan’s Offspring,” to the action short film entitled “The Ruthless Four,” as well as several features in the Suspense/Thriller genre and the martial arts action genre. Future films will span all genres, as we want to provide numerous choices for audiences to purchase, view and enjoy with friends and family.
Unlike the other companies who are limiting themselves to the golden 18 to 25 year old demographic, we plan on appealing to a much larger age range to garner the widest audience possible. Voilà NAM TV is broadening our audience by catering to a wide age range of 18 to 55 year old men and women, both will be thrilled by our films and other content. Along with the original cinema content, we will bring new, fresh and exciting musicians to the screen with our musical performance shows. Our first music show we produced was with the wonderful new Vietnamese band from Westminster, California called The WeBros band. The WeBros band performed several Vietnamese traditional and pop songs live during a thrilling and moving performance.
We here at Voilà NAM TV are proud to bring new, amazing, and highly entertaining movies, music, web series and more entertainment, all with an emphasis on a positive representation of the wonderful Vietnamese people and their cultures. We are proud to introduce the wonderfully talented and creative team behind the productions at Voilà NAM TV. Calvin Nguyen serves as producer, John R. Carpenter will be writing & directing the feature & short films, and Stefano Milla will be our Director of Photography on the productions we have scheduled to begin production soon.
